CANOPIES IN STOCK OR COMING SOON

Our focus here has always been on stocking Vector and then Javelin containers, because they were the hard piece to find when trying to put a system together. Lately though, and for several reasons, canopy manufacturers have been having trouble keeping certain canopies in stock. Specifically, for our clientele, the Sabre-3 has been impossible to find so we are starting to buy a few ourselves for stock and we will continue to do so until the situation improves. The canopies are listed here by size first and then by arrival date. A few are in stock but most are due over the next three months.
