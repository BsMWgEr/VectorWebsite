To Upload an Image:

  1. Click the button labeled 'browse' on the images section
  2. Select Image to upload

After following the 2 steps above you will have uploaded the selected image to your Websites main private storage (aka Amazon Cloud). This image is instantly viewable from the link provided after uploading. At the same time, this will automatically create a new database item under the Images section which (left sidebar in the Admin) is attachable to any Inventory Item. You can attach this image by selecting it from the dropdown when building a new Inventory Item (Newly uploaded items automatically propagate labeled as the file name you upload with example --> 'new_image.jpg'. You can find this Image information under the Images section on the left side of your admin page

All images uploaded under the images section are viewable to anyone on the internet. DO NOT upload private files or photos via this method! (There will be a private upload file section soon!)

TO ACCESS BUCKET DIRECTLY: Login to Bucket with the first link (S3 Bucket Login), after logged in click the second link (StockVectorRigs S3 Bucket) OR search for s3 in the AWS Console search bar at the top of page after login. Select 'stockvectorigs' one under the bucket tab
